Privacy Protocol

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

AllOSINT collects minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Username (codename), email address, and encrypted password
  • Usage Data: Search queries, tool usage, and session information
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our services
  • Improving user experience and functionality
  • Monitoring usage patterns and detecting abuse
  • Communicating important updates and security alerts
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Local Storage: User credentials are stored locally in your browser
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our systems are regularly audited and updated

4. OSINT Data Collection

When you use AllOSINT tools:

  • We only access publicly available information
  • Search queries are processed securely and not shared
  • Results are displayed to you only and not stored permanently
  • We comply with robots.txt and website terms of service

5. Cookies and Tracking

AllOSINT uses minimal cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Analytics to improve our service (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account termination. Search history and usage logs are automatically purged after 90 days.

8. Third-Party Services

AllOSINT integrates with external APIs for data gathering. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare DNS-over-HTTPS
  • Certificate Transparency Logs
  • Public WHOIS databases

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

AllOSINT is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

AllOSINT operates globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Protocol from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or through the platform. Continued use of AllOSINT after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us through our GitHub repository or email us at privacy@allosint.com

By using AllOSINT, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Protocol and agree to the collection and use of information as described.
